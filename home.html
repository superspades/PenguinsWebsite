Evegni Malkin is named the "First Star" of the week!

Malkin had 7 assists and 8 points last week to help move the Penguins back into first place in the Metropolitan Division. Malkin also currently leads all NHL players in assists and points for the month of November.

Pittsburgh Hosts USA Hockey Sled Classic

On Sunday November 24th the USA Hockey Sled Classic was held in the CONSOL Arena. The event gives physically disadvantaged individuals a chance to compete on the ice against other teams across the nation.
