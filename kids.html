Jr. Penguin Hockey Camp Sign Up

Camp is Saturday January 11th 7am - 6pm at the Civic Arena in Pittsburgh

Please submit the form below so we know how many kids we can expect at the camp. Fees are $100 per child payable by cash or check the morning of camp. All kids must have their own skates and hockey stick. Penguins Players will be teaching the kids the basics of hockey and setting up mock games. Players will be available to sign autographs for camp participants after the game. Please submit the form by December 20th so flyers with more information can be mailed.
